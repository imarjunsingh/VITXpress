About us

We Vitians, struggle to find taxis while travelling from campus to the nearby airports or railway stations. The taxi and autorickshaw fares are insane and really unfair. We need to find a way to optimise this problem to give feasible result.

So, I have created a website which let the students avail all the transport facilities at reasonable rates. To optimise the travel charges, I have used the current fuel rates and travel distances from major locations and providing services based on the client' s request.
